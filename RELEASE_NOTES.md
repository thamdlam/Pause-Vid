## Release v1.0.0-Beta: The Genesis Update

Welcome to the first public beta release of Pause Vid! This version lays the foundational architecture for video uploading, processing, and social interactions.

### ✨ What's New
* **Infinite Scroll Feed:** Implemented our proprietary content discovery algorithm.
* **Video Upload Pipeline:** Support for MP4/MOV compression up to 4K resolution.
* **Social Graphs:** Follow, like, comment, and share functionalities are live.
* **Creator Dashboard:** Track real-time views, watch time, and subscriber growth.

### 🐛 Bug Fixes & Optimisations
* Fixed a critical memory leak during continuous scrolling on Android devices.
* Optimised HLS video streaming bitrates for low-bandwidth networks.
* Resolved an authentication token expiration bug.
